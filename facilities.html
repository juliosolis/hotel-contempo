Facilities & services

What's one of the most important things while traveling? Relaxing!

What better way to relax than in our private Spa room exclusive for hotel guests. Our spa is designed to enhance a relaxing and renewing atmosphere for a perfect soothing experience.

Choose from a variety of massages, Sauna and our luxurious Jacuzzi, make your business or pleasure travel a delight for your body and mind.


Our spa room is open from 8:00 am to 5:00 pm, other hours available upon previous request.

Hotel Contempo provides transportation services at the best prices for our customers.

We can take you to Managua for your business meetings, shopping or any other activity. We also provide transfer to and from the airport for affordable prices, starting at $20.00. We can also take you to any place in Nicaragua and or in tour you select.

Airport transfer is included in our luxurious Contempo Deluxe rooms and Junior Suites.


Transfer with prior confirmation.



Multi day tours:


Made for the adventurous travelers who like to get the best out of their trips and explore the beauties of Nicaragua. We have prepared several packages of multi-day tours that include visits to places of interest combined with a stay at the beach where you can fish, surf, play golf, hike or simply enjoy the beautiful Pacific beaches of Nicaragua. Each package is fun, safe, and unforgettable!

One day tours:


One of the great things about Hotel Contempo is that there is a variety of places to see that are minutes away from the hotel. From Masaya to Granada, our one day tours are excellent to get a taste of the Nicaraguan culture. Feel free to ask reception for personalized tours that go in hand with your time and schedule.

About us

Our 32 stylish rooms, all with different designs inspired by Nicaraguan themes, boast distinctive elegance and the feel of a private residence, intimate and comfortable.
